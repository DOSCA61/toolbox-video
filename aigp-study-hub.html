Domain I · 16–20 questions
Understanding the Foundations of AI Governance
What AI governance is, core principles, why it matters, the AI lifecycle, stakeholders
Domain II
Understanding How Laws, Standards & Frameworks Apply to AI
EU AI Act, GDPR, NIST AI RMF, ISO 42001, sector-specific laws
Domain III
Understanding How to Govern AI Development
Risk management, data governance, technical docs, testing, validation (TEVV)
Domain IV
Understanding How to Govern AI Deployment and Use
Post-deployment monitoring, human oversight, incident response, provider vs deployer obligations
